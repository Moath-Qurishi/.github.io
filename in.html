مقابلة العمل

5 نصائح تجتاز مقابلة العمل بنجاح تتقدر تستخدمهم ChatGPT

اعمل بحث عن الشركة

✨I have an interview with [Company name]

Can you provide a SWOT* analysis based on their website [insert link] and suggest 2-3 interview questions that I can ask?""

حلل وصف الوظيفة

✨What are the key skills, qualifications and experiences that the company is seeking in a candidate?
Here is the job spec: [Insert job spec]

Then - Can you provide interview questions based on the job spec?

Please rank the questions based on key criteria for the role.

جيب إجابات الاسئلة المتوقعة

✨Based on my CV, please answer this question [insert the question].

Now Improve your answer.

Prompt: I have written a response to a question, and I would like your feedback on how to improve it [paste your answer].

قم بالبحث عن مسؤول التوظيف في الشركة

✨I will provide you with the LinkedIn profile link of my interviewer.

Please analyze and prepare questions I can ask [paste the URL].

Hype up the interviewer by focusing on their:

1. Career choice
2. Their biggest career wins
3. What advice would they give

ابعث رسالة شكر

✨Please create a structure for the thank you email, which I can send after the interview.

Note: Personalise the email by referring to key moments from the interview

Bonus: Send insightful articles related to the topic you discussed in the interview. You will be guaranteed to stand out.
